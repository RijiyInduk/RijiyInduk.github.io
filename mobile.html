  Started learning C# in 2019 and have been writing code in Visual Studio ever since. In 2022 I began developing my own game on Unity - and in April 2026 shipped it on Steam ("Fighters Guild"). In "Fighters Guild" I built from scratch: combat system, procedural level generation, inventory system, quests, skills system, audio system and localization in 4 languages. I consider video games a great form of entertainment. I enjoy the game development process, programming, level design, and game mechanics.
  Open to C# developer positions: game development, desktop, applied software.
